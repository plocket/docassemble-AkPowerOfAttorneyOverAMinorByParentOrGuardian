# Alaska Power Of Attorney Over A Minor By Parent Or Guardian

Allow a parent in Alaska to give an attorney-in-fact power of attorney over their children to make decisions about such things as medical care and education.

Part of the [US Delegation of Parental Rights project](https://github.com/SuffolkLITLab/50StateDelegationOfParentalRights/issues/1). See [the issue for the Alaska form](https://github.com/SuffolkLITLab/50StateDelegationOfParentalRights/issues/1).
